## What are Atoms?

Atoms are agents that can talk to anyone on voice or text in any language, in any voice. Imagine an AI that you can hire
to perform end-to-end tasks for your business. Atoms are highly configurable and can be setup to automate any workflow.

They are also extremely intelligent and can learn from mistakes to improve over time. Think of them as a AI employees
who are great at having conversations. All you have to do is plug in your business context and see the magic happen!
