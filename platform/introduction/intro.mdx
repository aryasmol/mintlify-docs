Atoms Platform is where you build, test, and deploy voice AI agents — without writing code. Design conversations visually, connect your data, pick a voice, and go live. The platform handles real-time speech processing, AI reasoning, and phone infrastructure.

---

## Agents

Two ways to build, depending on your use case.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Single Prompt" icon="message" href="/platform/single-prompt/overview">
    One prompt powers the entire conversation. Flexible, adaptive, handles unexpected turns. Best for support, FAQ, advisory.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Conversational Flow" icon="diagram-project" href="/platform/convo-flow/overview">
    Visual workflow with nodes and branches. Structured paths, predictable outcomes. Best for qualification, booking, intake.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
