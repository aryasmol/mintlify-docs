## **Core Use Cases**

### **1\. Patient Communication & Care Support**

- Automate appointment bookings, confirmations, and reminders.
- Provide prescription and medication adherence reminders.
- Offer 24/7 triage support and escalation for emergencies.

### **2\. Post-Visit & Discharge Follow-Up**

- Conduct recovery check-ins after discharge.
- Provide medication guidance and escalate concerns to doctors.
- Reduce readmission risks with timely follow-ups.

### **3\. Billing & Payment Assistance**

- Automate reminders for upcoming or overdue bills.
- Clarify insurance coverage and co-pays.
- Reduce administrative effort for finance teams.

### **4\. Patient Education & Engagement**

- Share information on vaccinations, wellness programs, and preventive care.
- Answer FAQs about treatments and recovery.
- Personalize education based on patient demographics.

### **5\. Internal Staff Operations**

- Act as an HR helpdesk for leave, payroll, and policies.
- Provide IT support for access and system issues.
- Send compliance training reminders to medical staff.
