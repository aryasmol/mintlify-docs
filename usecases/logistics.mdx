## **Core Use Cases**

### **1\. Shipment Tracking & Customer Support**

- Automate real-time shipment status queries across channels.
- Proactively notify customers about shipping, delays, or delivery updates.
- Resolve lost package or claim queries with minimal manual effort.

### **2\. Delivery Scheduling & Confirmation**

- Contact recipients to confirm or reschedule deliveries.
- Reduce failed attempts and optimize route planning.
- Capture proof of delivery and collect instant customer feedback.

### **3\. Payment & Collections**

- Send automated reminders for COD or freight payments.
- Confirm settlement intent securely.
- Improve cash flow by reducing defaults.

### **4\. Exception & Delay Management**

- Proactively communicate disruptions due to weather, customs, or supply chain issues.
- Provide alternatives and reduce inbound call spikes during disruptions.

### **5\. Fleet & Internal Operations**

- Automate communication with drivers for compliance checks, schedules, or urgent updates.
- Provide HR/IT support to employees to free up operations staff.
