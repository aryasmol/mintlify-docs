## **Core Use Cases**

### **1\. Customer Support & Engagement**

Banks handle millions of inbound queries daily such as balance checks, account re-opening, card blocking, loan inquiries, and KYC updates. Atoms can:

- Automate up to 99% of repetitive queries across voice and chat.
- Offer 24/7 multilingual, multi-channel support.
- Ensure seamless fallback to human agents for complex cases.
- Improve customer satisfaction while cutting operational costs.

---

### **2\. Sales, Lead Generation & Nurturing**

From credit cards to home loans and mutual funds, banks rely on large-scale sales. Atoms can:

- Qualify leads with smart questions and enrich CRM profiles.
- Run outbound campaigns at scale with personalized product pitches.
- Nurture cold leads with periodic engagement.
- Book appointments and hand off warm leads to sales teams.

---

### **3\. Collections & Payment Management**

Debt recovery is one of the most resource-intensive areas in banking. Atoms can:

- Send automated EMI, loan, and credit card repayment reminders.
- Handle multi-stage delinquency with tailored messages.
- Capture repayment intent securely and integrate with payment gateways.
- Improve repayment rates while reducing manual collection efforts.

---

### **4\. Security, Fraud & Compliance**

Trust and compliance are at the heart of banking. Atoms can:

- Notify customers of suspicious transactions instantly and verify legitimacy.
- Authenticate users via OTP or voice PIN
- Remind customers to update KYC and consent preferences.
- Support regulatory compliance with automated reminders and audit trails.

---

### **5\. Internal Operations & Efficiency**

Banks also need automation behind the scenes. Atoms can:

- Act as an HR helpdesk for leave, payroll, and policy queries.
- Serve as an IT helpdesk for access resets and downtime alerts
- Automate compliance training reminders for staff.
- Free up employees to focus on higher-value work.
