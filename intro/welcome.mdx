Atoms is a voice AI platform that turns phone calls into outcomes. Customers get instant help. Leads get qualified. Appointments get booked — all in real-time, at any scale, around the clock.

We built the entire stack from scratch. Pulse transcribes speech in 64ms. Electron reasons and responds in under 500ms. Lightning synthesizes studio-quality voice at 175ms. End-to-end, sub-800ms turn times — indistinguishable from human conversation.

### Our Models

| Model | What It Does | Performance |
|-------|--------------|-------------|
| **[Pulse](/intro/capabilities/voice-speech)** | Speech-to-Text | 32 languages, 64ms latency, 4.5% English WER |
| **[Electron](/intro/capabilities/voice-speech)** | Language Model | Voice-optimized SLM, sub-500ms reasoning |
| **[Lightning v3.1](/intro/capabilities/voice-speech)** | Text-to-Speech | 44kHz studio-grade, 175ms latency, voice cloning |
