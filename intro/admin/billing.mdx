# Billing & Pricing

Understand how Atoms pricing works, manage your subscription, and track your usage.

---

## Pricing Model

Atoms uses a **usage-based** pricing model:

| Component | How It's Measured |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Call minutes** | Total minutes of voice conversation |
| **Phone numbers** | Monthly fee per active number |
| **LLM tokens** | Tokens processed by the AI model |
| **Storage** | Knowledge base storage (GB) |

<Tip>New accounts get free credits to test the platform before adding a payment method.</Tip>

---

## Plans

| Plan | Best For | Includes |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Free Tier** | Testing & prototyping | Limited minutes, shared resources |
| **Starter** | Small teams | Base minutes, standard support |
| **Growth** | Growing businesses | Volume discounts, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Large organizations | Custom pricing, SLA, dedicated support |

---

## Managing Your Subscription

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. View current plan and usage
3. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel

---

## Payment Methods

We accept:
- **Credit/Debit Cards** — Visa, Mastercard, Amex
- **ACH/Bank Transfer** — US accounts (Enterprise)
- **Wire Transfer** — Enterprise customers
- **Invoice** — Enterprise with NET 30 terms

---

## Usage Tracking

Monitor your usage in real-time:

| Metric | Where to Find |
|--------|---------------|
| **Call minutes used** | Dashboard → Usage |
| **API calls** | Settings → API Usage |
| **Storage used** | Settings → Knowledge Bases |
| **Monthly spending** | Billing → Current Period |

---

## Billing Alerts

Set up alerts to avoid surprises:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing** → **Alerts**
2. Set thresholds (e.g., 80% of budget)
3. Choose notification method (email, Slack)

---

## Invoices & History

- View past invoices in **Billing** → **History**
- Download PDF invoices for accounting
- Access usage breakdowns per invoice
