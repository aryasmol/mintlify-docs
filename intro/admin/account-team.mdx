# Account & Team Management

Manage your Atoms workspace, invite team members, and configure roles and permissions.

---

## Workspaces

A **workspace** is your Atoms environment. It contains:
- Your agents
- Phone numbers
- Knowledge bases
- Campaigns
- Team members
- Billing

<Info>Most teams need just one workspace. Create additional workspaces for completely separate projects or clients.</Info>

---

## Team Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access, billing, delete workspace |
| **Admin** | Manage team, all features, no billing |
| **Editor** | Create/edit agents, campaigns, no team management |
| **Viewer** | View-only access to dashboards and logs |

---

## Inviting Team Members

1. Go to **Settings** → **Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter email address
4. Select role
5. Send invitation

Invitees receive an email with a link to join your workspace.

---

## Managing Members

| Action | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Change role** | Click member → Edit Role |
| **Remove member** | Click member → Remove |
| **Resend invite** | Click pending invite → Resend |

---

## Workspace Settings

Configure workspace-level settings:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Workspace name** | Display name for your workspace |
| **Default timezone** | Used for scheduling and analytics |
| **Notification preferences** | Email alerts for campaigns, errors |
| **API access** | Enable/disable API access |

Enterprise plans support SSO via:
- **SAML 2.0**
- **OAuth / OIDC**
- **Google Workspace**
- **Microsoft Entra ID**
